About Our Service


The food served at both restaurant is one of the highlights of your stay, and we serve a mix of Indian and international cuisine.
Dining can be as formal or informal as you wish, from full four-course gourmet meals to healthy meals with the children.
Meals can be themed, traditional or a mix! Ask us for some ideas or give us yours.
All meals at The Lodge are served communally around a shared table, but it is possible to organise private dinners in some rooms on request.

Favourite Dish


We may not be able to travel to every country on Earth, but a great way to get a taste of a culture is to sample its signature dishes. Here’s a selection of popular dishes you shouldn’t miss..

Berry Rhuburb Fool Cake

A "fool" is a British dessert that's usually made with custard. This is a modified, quicker version I created. My kids love it because it doesn't taste like rhubarb—so I guess it's well named! —Cheryl Miller, Fort Collins, Colorado.....

Triple Cheese cake

What could be better than a brownie crust layered with chocolate cheesecake
and topped with chocolate ganache?
These cheesecake bars will satisfy even the biggest chocolate lovers out there. —Andrea Price, Grafton, Wisconsin.....
Pumpkin Pecan Custard

My family loves pumpkin pie flavor, but this is a delicious, creamy, healthier alternative
